# Bold Canonical Budget Categories Reference

**Source of truth:** Bold's "טמפלט תקציב 01.xls" (in use since 2010) and "תבנית מתודולוגית לאירועים 4" (checklist of ~140 line items, in use since 2004, updated 2011).

**Purpose:** Stage 5 of `bold-proposal-builder` MUST pick every budget line from this canonical tree. The skill does not invent categories. When a Stage 4 deliverable doesn't match any line here, the skill flags it and asks Hemi which existing line to use or whether to add a new one under "שונות".

## The spine: 6 top-level categories

Every budget line maps to exactly one of these 6. These are the rows that carry the category name in column 7 (סעיף תקציבי) of `budget.xlsx`:

1. **כללי** (General)
2. **תקשור מקדים** (Pre-event communications)
3. **מיתוג ושילוט** (Branding & signage)
4. **טכני** (Technical)
5. **כח אדם ולוגיסטיקה** (Staff & logistics)
6. **שונות** (Miscellaneous)

Category order is fixed. Do not reorder.

## Production fee

Single line labeled "דמי ארגון והפקה" at 15% of subtotal. This is Bold's margin. It is:
- NOT a separate category
- NOT called "Bold רווח" or "margin"
- Appears once at the end of the main budget section, and once at the end of the conditional section if conditionals exist
- Always 15% by default; Hemi can override at Stage 5 gate 6

## Conditional items section ("אופציות")

Items the client opts in/out of separately. They:
- Live in a second section of the same sheet (rows 32+ in `budget.xlsx`)
- Have their own 15% production fee line
- Have their own total ("סה\"כ חשבונית")
- Are categorized under כללי only in the conditional section (flat list, not subdivided by the 6 canonicals)
- Examples: extra hour of bar service, upgrade the lighting rig, add pyrotechnics
