# Hemi Michaeli, Inferred Visual & Conceptual Preferences

This file is built up event by event. It accumulates patterns from Stage 3 (brand heart) and Stage 4a (visualization) selections. Claude reads it at the start of every Stage 3 and Stage 4a session to calibrate the three directions it proposes.

**Principle:** Learn to propose better three-direction sets, but never collapse all three to safe choices. One of the three is always a stretch.

## Stretch policy (guard against over-fitting)

Claude will monitor its own proposals. If in the last 5 sessions it has NOT offered a direction that Hemi later rejected, something is wrong: Claude is only proposing safe directions.

Correction: immediately insert a deliberate stretch direction in the next session, even if Hemi likely won't pick it. The stretch exists to keep Claude honest about range.

Rejection rate target: 15-35% of individual directions (not whole sets). Below 15% means the range is too narrow. Above 35% means Claude isn't learning.
